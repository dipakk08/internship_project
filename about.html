Porsche

1. Porsche 911

Engine: 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged flat-six engine, up to 640 hp.

Transmission: 8-speed dual-clutch automatic (PDK).

Performance: 0-60 mph in 2.6 seconds.

Features: Advanced infotainment system, premium sound, and driver assistance features.

2. Porsche Taycan

Engine: Dual electric motors producing up to 750 hp.

Transmission: 2-speed transmission for rear axle.

Performance: 0-60 mph in 3.0 seconds (Turbo model).

Features: 16.8-inch curved display, adaptive air suspension, and premium interior materials.

3. Porsche Macan

Engine: 2.0L Turbocharged inline-4 engine with 265 hp.

Transmission: 7-speed dual-clutch automatic (PDK).

Performance: 0-60 mph in 6.4 seconds.

Features: Adaptive cruise control, sport seats, and advanced connectivity options.

4. Porsche Cayenne

Engine: 3.0L V6 engine producing 335 hp.

Transmission: 8-speed automatic transmission.

Performance: 0-60 mph in 5.9 seconds.

Features: Luxurious interior, adaptive air suspension, and advanced safety systems.
